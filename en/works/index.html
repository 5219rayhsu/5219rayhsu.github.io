Works

Projects

Three ongoing long-term projects. Interweaving film, performance and AI-generated imagery to address Taiwan’s examination system, identity politics and contemporary idolatry.

  1. Work 01

    Chaos Body Shop

    無序身體販賣所

    A long-term mixed-media project in three series: Photographic Collage, AI ID Photo Series, and the Diamond Sutra rearranged in Bopomofo. Comprising 456 AI-generated ID photos, 7 photographic collages and a hand-copied sutra, it responds to idolatry, the truth of information and identity politics in the age of AI. When I Grow Up, a single work from the AI ID Photo Series, won the Gold Prize in the Photography Category at the National Art Exhibition R.O.C.

    Mixed media · 2025–

  2. Work 02

    Answer

    答案

    Examination-themed portraits and installations shot on 4×5 large-format black-and-white film, looking back on discipline and selfhood under Taiwan’s college-entrance system. HSU’s first solo exhibition opened at Keelung Museum of Fine Arts in 2025.

    4×5 B&W film · Solo 2025

  3. Work 03

    Medicine

    心藥

    Self-imagery combining AI tarot and performance. It premiered in 2025 at beholdingcell as “HSU Che-jui: 22nd Birthday Special Exhibition.”

    AI Tarot · Performance · 2025
