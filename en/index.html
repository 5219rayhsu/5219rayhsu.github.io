About

About the artist

HSU Che-jui (b. 2003, Taiwan) began photography on May 20, 2022 and started making art in December 2023. Rooted in conceptual and performance practice, his media span 4×5 black-and-white film, AI-generated imagery, tarot and Bajiquan.

  • MentorsCHIANG Li-hua, YAO Jui-chung, YANG Chen-hao, CHANG Shao-chiang, HAN Chi-hsiang
  • StudyingMFA, Contemporary Visual Culture and Practice, NTUA; MITx MicroMasters in Statistics and Data Science
  • MemberNTU Photo Club; Society of Photographic Museum and Culture of Taiwan; AVAT; Taiwan Photographic Cultural Heritage Association
